Post your Loads in Seconds - No More Spreadsheets or Calls!

Just post once and notify all your transporters.

  • Ditch Excel and endless follow-ups. Just post once and notify all your transporters.
  • Add pickup & delivery locations, material details, and truck type
  • Set bidding time, payment terms, assign transporter groups → Hit “Post”

Manage Bids or Assign Trucks — All in One Place

Once your load is live, you can choose how to proceed:

  • Assign to Contract Transporters: Transporter adds truck & driver details. Load is confirmed and scheduled.
  • Accept Bids from Others: Review bids, select the best. Transporter adds truck & driver details. Load is confirmed and scheduled.

Track Loading & Stay Updated in Real-Time

Say goodbye to ‘Where is the truck?’ follow-ups.

  • Truck driver logs In & Out time during loading.
  • Live tracking via:
    – SIM tracking (with driver consent)
    – Or FASTag-based tracking
  • Get real-time updates, no need for constant follow-ups.

End-to-End Seamless Delivery & Document Upload

Once delivery is done, all required documents are uploaded for a seamless handover.

  • Uploads Proof of Delivery (POD) instantly
  • Includes LR/GR, weight slip, and invoice slip
  • Ensures quick verification and organized trip completion

Everything You Need to Know

How to register on Loadsor.com as a Transporter / Broker.

Visit LoadSor.com on browser or download mobile app.
Click on "SignUp".
Click on "Free Signup" against the Transporter/Booking Agent option.
Fill up all the mandatory details and upload necessary documents.
Finalize the registration by processing a Rs 10/- transaction.

How to complete KYC process

After completing SighUp process your respected account will be created On the homepage of your LoadSor account click on "Documents" visible in the left menu.
Upload all the necessary documents to complete KYC

How to do Email verification

Enter your Email ID during the SignUp process
Upon successful SignUp, you will receive a verification email.
Open your respective mailbox and click on the "Confirm email" button to complete the process.

How to see load posts or orders?

Login to LoasSor.com On the homepage of your LoadSor account, click on "Load Board" in the left column.
The new page will display all the open/pending orders.

How to quote ?

Login to LoadSor.com On the homepage of your LoadSor account, select "Load Board." Review the open order status, including source and destination cities and all necessary details.
Click on the "Quote Now" button.
Enter your rate and unit in Full Truck Load(FTL) or Per Metric Ton(PMT).

Can I Change my submitted quote?

Yes, a broker or transporter can change the quotation only if the new quotation is less than the previous one.

How will I know if my quote is lowest or not?

Yes, a broker or transporter can change the quotation only if the new quotation is less than the previous one.

How will I know if my quote is lowest or not?

Once the quotation is given it will give you push notification regarding your quote status (i.e. L1, L2 or L3) Alternatively, click on Check L1 button to check status of your quotation

How to Post a Load?

Login to Loadsor.com
On the homepage of your Loadsor account.
Select post a load on the left hand menu.
Click load post button on top of the right hand side.
Fill all details and submit post.

Can I Cancel my submitted quote?

You can cancel your quote easily.
To cancel your quote, simply click the "Cancel Quote" button.
Please note that after canceling your quote, you can only requote after a waiting period of 60 seconds.
This ensures a smooth and efficient processing of quote requests.
